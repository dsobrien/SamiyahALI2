About Us

Samiyah Ali, has over 15 years of experience assisting families with life events from A-Z. She has been the connector and solution to putting her client’s financial pieces together. Starting with a career in real estate sales, in addition she has migrated to a career of sales in Insurance. On her journey she assists clients with obtaining assets to teaching and providing the tools and services to maintain them assets as well.

Samiyah uses her experience and foresight to proactively address details before they become a problem. Her mission is to provide the values in Financial Services, while granting the best solutions, building strong, and long lasting relationships.

WE BELIEVE IN FINANCIAL SECURITY

Over the years, she has become the go to resource for Health and Wealth management products. Products such as Term and Whole life, Health insurance, Retirement plans, Credit preparedness, College savings plans, Final expenses, Mortgage Insurance, Supplemental care as her portfolio grows. Being licensed in multiple states and contracted with most of your major carriers she has the ability to structure a plan that works best for you and your family, she is your network.

She studied Business Management/Marketing at the University of Phoenix. Samiyah comes from a family of entrepreneurs where she inherited her innovative thinking. Samiyah has assisted Sanford, Florida with youth programs, financial education that has contributed growth and development to the community. In her spare time, Samiyah enjoys time with friends and family, engaging in event planning, networking and, seeking adventure in the outdoors, while keeping a good book on site!
